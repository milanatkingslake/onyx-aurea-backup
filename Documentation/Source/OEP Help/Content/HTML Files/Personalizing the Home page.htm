Personalizing Home Page

The Home page is your personalized starting point for using ONYX.

You decide the kind of information that you want to display on the Home page and where to position it. Your Home page can consist of lists of important customers, incidents, tasks, and work tickets, for instance, or lists of the records that you access most frequently. You can also configure your Home page to display your Outlook calendar.

The Companies and Individuals section of the Home page consists of Favorite and Top 10 (most recently updated) customers. Incident, task, and work ticket sections are based on Task Manager queries that you create. The Navigator Filters section displays the results for the Navigator queries you select.

What would you like to do?

  • View my Home page
  • Configure the layout of my Home page
  • Configure the Companies and Individuals section
  • Configure the Navigator Filters section
  • Configure the incident, task, and work ticket sections
  • Configure the reports section

View my Home page

To view the Home page, click from the Navigation bar. If you want to view the Home page whenever you log on to ONYX , set your Startup View user preference accordingly.

Based on your configuration, the Home page may include some or all of the following sections:

  • Companies and Individuals
  • Incidents
  • Tasks
  • Work Tickets
  • Calendar
  • Navigator Filters
  • Reports

Click and to expand and contract each section.

Configure the layout of my Home page

To configure the layout of your Home page

  1. Click to open the Select Items window where you can select the content to display on your Home page. Click the >> and << buttons to move highlighted sections between the Available and Selected list.
  2. Click the Move Up and Move Down buttons to set the relative positions of the sections on the Home page.
  3. Click to save your settings and close the window. The Home page layout changes immediately to reflect your changes.

Configure the Companies and Individuals section

The Companies and Individuals section includes:

  • Favorite Companies
  • Favorite Individuals
  • Top 10 Companies
  • Top 10 Individuals

Favorite Companies and Favorite Individuals contain customer records that you have specified as Favorites.

Top 10 Companies and Top 10 Individuals contain recently updated records for which you are an internal contact.

Designating a customer as a Favorite

For a customer record to display as a Favorite, you must specify yourself as an internal contact with a contact type called "Favorite."

To designate a customer a favorite

  1. Search for the customer record that you want to designate as a favorite.
  2. Select the Internal Contacts tab and click .
  3. On the Add Contact page, select the Favorites contact type.
  4. Click to make yourself an internal contact.

The customer record appears in the Favorite section of your Home page the next time it is refreshed.

Designating a customer as Top 10

The Top 10 section on the Home page enables you to view the ten most recently updated customer records. A customer record appears in the Top 10 section of your Home page when:

  • You are an internal contact for the customer
  • There is an incident associated with the customer
  • The customer's incident, task, or work notes information is changed

Updates to the demographic and contact information on a customer record, such as changes to a phone number or address, do not place the customer in the Top 10 section.

Configure the Navigator Filters section

You can configure the Navigator Filters section to display the results of your most common Navigator queries. The Home page displays the maximum number of rows that meet the filter's search as specified in the Navigator Search Results Selection and Administration window.

To configure Navigator filter results to appear on your Home page

  1. Create and save one or more Navigator queries.

When saving a filter, be sure to select the Is HomePage check box to be able to select the filter on the Home page.

  1. Click to return to the Home page.
  2. Configure the layout of your Home page so that the Navigator Filters section is displayed.
  3. In the Navigator Filters section, click .
  4. In the Homepage Queries window, expand the Public Queries and Private Queries folders and select the queries to display.
  5. Save your selection and close the Homepage Queries window. The results for the selected filters are displayed on your Home page.
  6. Click on a filter header to view the filter results in full screen mode.
  7. Click to refresh and display the updated filter results.

Configure incident, task, and work ticket sections

You can configure your Home page to display the results of your most common Task Manager queries for Incidents, Tasks, and Work Tickets. The Home page displays the first ten items that meet the filter's search criteria, ordered according to the filter's sort criteria.

To configure Incidents, Tasks, and Work Tickets to appear on your Home page

  1. Create and save one or more Task Manager queries.
  2. Click to return to the Home page.
  3. Configure the layout of your Home page.
  4. On your Home page, in the Incidents, Tasks, or Work Tickets section, click .
  5. Click the >> and << buttons to move highlighted sections between the Available and Selected views. The selected queries are included on the Home page under the respective section.

Configure the reports section

You can configure your Home page to display the BI for SSRS reports that you most often use.

To configure reports to appear on your Home page

  1. Use SQL Server Reporting Services to create the reports you need. For information on doing this, see Using BI for Microsoft SSRS.
  2. Configure the layout of your Home page so that the Reports section is displayed.
  3. On your Home page, in the Reports section, click .
  4. Click the >> and << buttons to move highlighted sections between the Available and Selected views. The selected reports are included on the Home page.
  5. On the report header, choose an option to view a report.
  • Click to view the report in full screen mode. Click to switch back to the normal view.
  • Click to view the report in full height mode. Click to switch to a half height view.
  • Click to view the report in full width mode. Click to switch to a half width view.
  1. Click to refresh the report.
