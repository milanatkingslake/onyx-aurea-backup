About Email Templates

Email templates contain prewritten message text that can be inserted into unsent emails as needed. Templates are useful for creating reusable messages for commonly sent business communications.

The text from a template can be used as the entire body text of an email or as a portion to build around. The text from the template can be edited before the email has been sent. A template can also extract data from the database and insert it into the email text.

Templates are editable from the Template Maintenance window, which is accessible from the Navigation bar.
