About Calendar

The Calendar feature enables you to create appointments in Microsoft Outlook and access them in OEP. You can also create appointments from within OEP, add notes, and associate your appointments with a customer or an incident. You can view appointments by day, week, or month without ever leaving the OEP user interface.

To view your Outlook calendar and appointments from within OEP, click in the Navigation bar to launch Outlook, or add Outlook to the Home Page layout. You can also view appointments associated with customers or incidents from the Appointments or History tab on the PowerPage or incident information page.

Microsoft Outlook must be configured and running on the client machine before you use the Calendar feature.
