About Messenger

Messenger enables you to send messages to other OEP users to communicate issues regarding customers, tasks, and incidents. Messenger behaves much like an internal email system, and you can attach links to OEP entities that recipients can open directly from within a message.

OEP can be configured through the General Preferences to notify you when you have a new message.

To open the Messenger window click , and then click Messenger. A window opens and lists the messages that are in your Messenger inbox.
