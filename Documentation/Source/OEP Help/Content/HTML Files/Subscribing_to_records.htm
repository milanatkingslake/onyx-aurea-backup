Subscribing to data

You can subscribe to several different types of items in OEP. Your system administrator determines the item types that are available for subscription. You can identify items that are available for subscription by looking for the subscription icon . Items to which you are already subscribed display in the item header or toolbar.

To subscribe to an item:

 Click on the header or toolbar for the item.

 For a quote, click on the Quote toolbar.

 Click on the toolbar to subscribe items to others using Advanced Subscription.

To subscribe to multiple items at once:

  1. Run search in either Advanced Search or Task Manager.
  2. Select the items that you want to subscribe from the result list.
  3. Click on the toolbar.

To subscribe items to other users:

 If you are an advanced subscriber, you can use Advanced Subscription to subscribe items from other users.
