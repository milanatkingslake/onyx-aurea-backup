Adding and Modifying Appointments

You can use Outlook to add, modify, or delete existing appointments without ever leaving OEP. Outlook is accessible from the Home Page, and from the in the navigation bar.

For help using Outlook, consult the Outlook Help from within the Outlook application.

Adding new appointments

From your Outlook calendar in OEP:

  1. Open Outlook by selecting the Calendar section on the Home Page, or clicking   in the toolbar.
  2. Double-click the Outlook schedule area. An Outlook New Appointment window opens.
  3. Schedule the appointment as you normally schedule Outlook appointment.
  4. Click Save and Close to close the appointment window.

From the Appointments tab:

  1. Click in the appointments tab toolbar. The Outlook New Appointment window opens.
  2. Schedule the appointment as you normally schedule any Outlook appointment.
  3. Click Save and Close to close the appointment window and return to OEP.

Your appointment automatically appears in the Outlook application, and on the Appointments tab of the associated customer PowerPage and/or incident information page.

Modifying appointments

  1. Launch Outlook by opening the Calendar section on the Home Page, or clicking in the toolbar.
  2. Double-click the appointment you want to change or delete.
  3. Modify the appointment as you normally modify any Outlook appointment.
  4. Click Save and Close to close the appointment window.
