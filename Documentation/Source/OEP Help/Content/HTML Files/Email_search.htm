Email Search

Using the Email tab on the Search pane, you can perform email searches.

To perform an email search:

  1. Enter search delimiters in the QuickSearch text box and click .

The Search window appears.

  1. Click the Email tab.
  2. Specify search criteria.
  3. Click on the Search toolbar.

The results of the search appear below the search form.

  1. Click an email in the results list to open that email in the email information window.

Use the supported search delimiters to broaden searches on the description field.

When you execute the email search, if only one email is found that matches the search criteria, OEP automatically opens the email information window for that incident.
