About Advanced Subscriptions

All OEP users who have permission to use OEP-Offline can subscribe to items for themselves. Additionally, your system administrator can designate certain OEP end-users as advanced subscribers. Advanced subscribers are OEP users with permissions to use OEP-Offline, and who have also been authorized to subscribe to items on behalf of other users using Advanced Subscription. This is useful in scenarios when you want to allow a single individual to designate other users to have access to specific data in OEP-Offline.

In addition to being able to subscribe to items for others, advanced subscribers also have access to additional functionality in Subscription Review.  Subscription Review allows subscribers to review all of the items to which they have subscribed, and also enables advanced subscribers to review the items to which other OEP-Offline users have subscribed.

Only those users who are designated Advanced Subscribers can subscribe items on the behalf of other OEP-Offline users.
