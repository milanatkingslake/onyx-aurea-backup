Selecting Contacts

Each customer product can be assigned a contact. Any individual record in the database can be assigned as a contact for a product.

To select a contact for a product:

  1. Click next to the Contact field in the product information window.

The Search for a Primary Contact window appears.

  1. Enter criteria into the form and click to execute the search.

If the search returns more than one individual, a list appears in the bottom half of the window.

  1. Click an individual record in the results list to mark the person as a contact for the product.

The window automatically closes and the individual's name appears in the Contact field in the product information window.

If the search returns only one individual record, the search window closes and the person is automatically assigned as contact for the product.

Remarks

A contact can be removed from the product by clicking . The field clears and the contact information is no longer linked.

Within the Search for a Primary Contact window, you can:

  • Click to clear the search criteria.
  • Click to hide (contract) the search criteria pane, which is useful when your search returns a long list of results.
  • Click to expand search criteria pane that you previously contracted.
