About Preferences

You can configure OEP to better meet your personal workflow needs by making changes to your system preferences. There are preference settings for the overall behavior of the application, and there are settings for the default values that are given to new incident, task, and work ticket records.

To open the User Preferences window, click and then click User Preferences. The preference settings are divided between a series of tabs. Each tab represents a different section of OEP. Click a tab to view the preferences associated with a section.

Click to save your changes to your settings.
