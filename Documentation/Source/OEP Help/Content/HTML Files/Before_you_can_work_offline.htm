Before you can work offline

Before you can work offline, you must configure your Microsoft Outlook profile for offline use and set your synchronization preferences. See the OEP-Offline Getting Started Guide and be sure you have completed all tasks required to configure your Outlook profile for offline use.

Once you have configured your Outlook profile, determine which data you want to access offline and subscribe to that data in OEP. To initiate synchronization, Outlook must be running while you are connected to the network, and you must initiate synchronization appropriately for your Outlook version and profile configuration. After Outlook synchronization is complete, launch OEP-Offline to access your subscribed data. Your changes will be saved locally until you reconnect to the network and synchronize with OEP again.

Connection modes and synchronization are configured in Outlook. See the Help for your version of Outlook for more information.
