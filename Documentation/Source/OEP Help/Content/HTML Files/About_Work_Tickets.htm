About Work Tickets

Work tickets represent activities or tasks that can be linked to almost any other entity in OEP, including customers, incidents, and other work tickets. Work tickets contain information regarding the work to be completed, and provide tracking and resolution information. Users can also type notes as they monitor the progress of a work ticket.

Work ticket data is accessible from the Navigation Bar in two places.

  • Click Work Ticket within the Add New... submenu to open a blank work ticket in a separate window.
  • Click Work Tickets within the Customer submenu to display the list of work tickets that are linked to the active customer record.
