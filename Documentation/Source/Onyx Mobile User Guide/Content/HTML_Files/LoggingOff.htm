Logging out

To log out of from Onyx Mobile App

  1. Go to the Home page either by double-clicking Home on your iOS device, or by clicking Home from the Flow Menu on your Android device.
  2. Do the following:
  • If you are using an iOS device, click Logout.
  • If you are using an Android phone, click Logout from the Flow Menu.

To log out of from Onyx Mobile Web

  1. Go to the Home page either by clicking Home.
  2. Click the Log out icon .
