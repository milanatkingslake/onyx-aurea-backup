Viewing Records

Use the following pages to view your customer and related information. The fields available to you for viewing are configured by your Onyx System Administrator based on your organization's requirements. Click a link for more information on using the page.

  • Home
  • My Favorite Companies
  • My Favorite Individuals
  • My Recent Customers
  • My Recent Incidents
  • Web Links
  • Company Details
  • Individual Details
  • Incident Details
  • Task Details
  • WorkTicket Details
  • Forecast Details
  • Quote Details

Tap the respective fields in a screen to navigate to another Onyx Mobile screen, or to perform an action on your iOS or Android device. These fields are indicated by an arrow mark to the right of the screen. However, to navigate to the desired screen or feature, tap the field and not the arrow mark.
