Home

Use the Home page to navigate to other screens of the application where you can view lists of recent and favorite customers, and add new customer records.

You can view any mobile bookmark you had configured in Navigator. If your System Administrator has configured user-specific mobile bookmarks for tasks and incidents, you can also view these bookmarks on your Home page. Click a mobile bookmark to view a list of records that meet the search criteria for the corresponding Navigator query.

 

Note: To revert to the Home page from any other screen:

  • On your iOS device, double-tap Home.
  • On your Android phone, tap Home from the Flow menu.
  • On the Onyx Mobile Web, click the Home button.
