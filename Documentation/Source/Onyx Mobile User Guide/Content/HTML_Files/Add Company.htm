Add or Edit Company Records

Add a new company to your Onyx system or edit an existing company in Onyx. The fields available to add or modify are configured by your administrator. Required fields are denoted with the * symbol next to the field name.

Note:

Onyx Mobile App: Based on the device you are using, the appearance of Telephone and Email Address fields will vary.
  • For iOS, options are provided to select the primary Telephone and Email Address fields.
  • For Android, check boxes are provided to select the primary Telephone and Email Address fields.

Onyx Mobile Web: Check boxes are provided to select the primary Telephone and Email Address fields.

To add or edit company records

  1. On the Home page, click Add Company.
  2. To edit an existing company, open the Company record, and then click Edit.
  3. Type the company name and URL.
  4. To add a parent company or a primary contact for the company, click the Link icon . The Search page appears.
  • Type the search criteria and run the search.
  • In the Search Result List page, select a customer record to link to the record you are modifying.
  1. Click a field to type or select values. For drop-down lists or date selection fields, a new page appears. Select the desired value, and then click to confirm your selection.
  2. If you have multiple email or phone fields, click the respective option or select the check box to set it as the primary email and phone.
  3. To save the company record, click Save on the top-right of the page. The record appears in the My Recent Customers List page.
  • To discard your entries, click Back on the top-left of the page.
  1. To add internal and external contacts to the record, open the Details page of the newly saved record from the My Recent Customers list, and add contacts as desired.
