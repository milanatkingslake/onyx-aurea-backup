Introduction

Onyx Mobile is a native application for iOS and Android devices and Onyx Mobile App is a cross browser application. These integrate with Onyx application and you can manage your customer interactions while you are on the move. Both these applications facilitate you to access your customer and contact information on your devices.

Using Onyx Mobile App and Onyx Mobile Web, you can also:

  • View a list of favorite customers.
  • View a list of recent customers and incidents.
  • View details of existing customers, incidents, task, work ticket, quote, and forecast records.
  • Search for existing customer and incident records. You can also search Individuals and Companies separately.
  • Add new incident and task records.
  • Modify details for existing customer records, such as phone number, email, address, as well as primary, external, and internal contacts.
  • Modify details for existing incident records, such as contacts, work notes, status, and priority.
  • Modify details for existing task records, such as contacts, work notes, status, and priority.
  • Send emails and call customers from within the application.
  • View directions to your customer's location applicable.

This feature is available only on Onyx Mobile App.

  • Launch the Detail screen through a URL without going to the Home screen.
  • Quickly update certain fields of the entities through the DoMore section on the Detail screen.
  • Retrieve the list of various records by scanning the bar code of a product.

This feature is available only on Onyx Mobile App.

  • Send email, send signed email, and store attachment on Sales / Service / Support incidents.

This feature is available only on Onyx Mobile App.

  • View lists of incidents, products, and tasks associated with a customer.
  • View lists of incidents and tasks assigned to the logged on user.
  • View Navigator queries you had saved as Bookmarks.

In Onyx Mobile App, an activity indicator appears, indicating the progress of your activity. To avoid unexpected results, do not perform any other action until the activity indicator stops.

Downloading Onyx Mobile App

You can download the Onyx Mobile App either from App Store (for iOS devices) or Play Store (for Android devices).
