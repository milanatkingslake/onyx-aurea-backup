Increasing Allocated Application Storage Space

When you log on to Onyx, critical customer information is cached on your computer so that it can be retrieved quickly later when you need it. Depending on the size of your database, caching may take some time, thus increasing the time taken to log on. A progress bar provides a visual indication of the status of the caching process.

Your computer comes with a default application storage space of 1 MB to store cached data when you access websites. If your Onyx database contains a huge number of records, Onyx users may be prompted to increase this allocation to enable successful caching during logon. Based on the amount of data being cached, you will be asked to increase the allocated space in order to continue using Onyx.

On clicking the Increase Allocation button, the user will get a confirmation prompt to increase the required storage space .
