Search by Email Address

You can perform searches based on a Contact’s email address (for example, john.doe@email.com).  The Contact record(s) that match the email address provided is displayed in the Customer Panel below the Search bar.

Typically only one Contact record will be expected to be associated to an email address.  In case multiple records exist for one email address in the database, all matching records are displayed in the Customer Panel.

For more information about how multiple matches are handled from the auto-resolved email address, see Multiple Resolutions for Email Addresses

Note: Unlike the name searches, the email address searches are exact match searches.
