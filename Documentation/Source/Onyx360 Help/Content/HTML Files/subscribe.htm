Subscribe/Unsubscribe Email Conversation

One click action allows users to track the email conversation in Onyx directly from the Onyx 360 panel. 

When tracked, the email and all future replies to it are automatically saved to Onyx as they are sent and received by the user.  In other words, if a user replies to a tracked email, the reply is automatically saved in Onyx.  When the contact responds to the reply, it is also tracked automatically.

An email message that is set to be tracked in Onyx will be indicated on the email window and all future emails received on this thread as shown below.

The tracked email thread may be viewed or accessed in Onyx on the Contact’s record, from the Email tab on the PowerPage.
