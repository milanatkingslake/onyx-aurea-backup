Contact Details

The Contact Panel lists the details of the associated Contact record from the Onyx CRM database in the Onyx Outlook Add-in.  The detail information listed in this section is configurable to include various data points from the contact’s record in Onyx. 

The Contact information is retrieved from the Onyx database and relevant details are displayed in this section based on a predetermined configuration.  The configuration is controlled by your System Administrator and can include as many or as little detail as required by your business. 

Note: The Contact detail view may also be modified to include custom fields that are specific to your Onyx implementation as long as the custom fields have been added to the individual object’s properties list as per OEAS Development Standards.

For information about creating a custom property on a core object in Onyx, refer to the OEAS Developer’s Guide.
