Synchronize Existing Outlook Contacts to Onyx

Existing Contact records in a user’s Outlook Address Book may be uploaded to the Onyx database through the simple action of clicking the Save/Sync selected in Onyx button from the Onyx 360 panel with the Contact’s record selected.

Contacts that are not previously synchronized with Onyx will display a message as shown in the image below:

Users can click the Save/Sync button to save the contact in Onyx as an individual record.  The save action uploads the Contact’s name, email address, phone number(s), and address details to the Onyx CRM database.
