Multiple Resolutions for Email Addresses

Typically, only one email address will be associated with one customer record.  In case multiple customer records are returned from the look-up, Onyx 360 automatically displays the most recent record as the preferred customer and displays the number of records matching the email address next to the customer information.

Clicking on the parenthesized duplicate count displays all the matching customer records and the user has the option to change the record being displayed by choosing from this list.  Onyx 360 automatically remembers the user’s selection and from this point forward, the email address will automatically be resolved to the user’s preferred record.

Note: Onyx 360 will remember the user selection only for the current session. The selection will be lost when Onyx 360 is disabled or Outlook is restarted.

Users are responsible for fixing the duplicate data by merging the records in Onyx CRM.
