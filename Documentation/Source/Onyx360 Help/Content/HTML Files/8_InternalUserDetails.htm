Internal User Details

Internal employee email addresses in an email are automatically looked up from the Onyx users table and pertinent information about the employee’s Onyx user record is displayed on the Onyx 360 side-panel under the Internal Users section.

The information displayed in this section is retrieved from the Onyx database and displayed based on a predetermined configuration.  The configuration is controlled by your System Administrator and may be configured to include additional information regarding internal users as required. 

Note: If an Onyx 360 user has the option to Show Resolved Employee Records turned on in their User Preferences, the internal employee email addresses are resolved against the Onyx individual table and displayed as customer records in the side-panel.
