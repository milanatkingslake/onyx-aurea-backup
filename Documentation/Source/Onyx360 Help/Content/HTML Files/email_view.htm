Email View

Contacts that were successfully matched in Onyx to the email addresses in the From, To, and Cc email fields on the selected email are displayed in this section. 

The Contact information is color coded to indicate the nature of the contact record within the system.

  • Customer contacts are color coded as Blue
  • Internal users are color coded as Gray
  • If internal users are maintained in Onyx as individual records, the records will display as a customer contact.  Users have the option to turn this on or off from Settings.
  • Unresolved email addresses (that is, email addresses that do not correspond to a Contact record in Onyx) will be color coded as Red.  Please note that these records will not have any associated Onyx data. 

Users may optionally choose to not include unresolved contacts (that is, records that do not exist in the Onyx database) in this display, by selecting the Hide Unresolved Contacts check box.  This option hides all names or email addresses shown in red.
