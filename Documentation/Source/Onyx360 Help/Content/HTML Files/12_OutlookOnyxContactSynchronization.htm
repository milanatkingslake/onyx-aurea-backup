Outlook/Onyx Contact Synchronization

Onyx 360 supports two-way synchronization of Contacts between Onyx and Outlook.  Contacts created locally in a user’s Outlook address book may be uploaded to the Onyx database, and future changes to these contacts made in Onyx may be downloaded to the user’s Outlook address book.

To create a new Contact record through the Onyx 360 panel, see New Contact Workflow.
