Contact Detail Lists

The Contact panel lists the details of the associated Contact record from the Onyx CRM database in the Onyx Outlook Add-in.

The Contact panel may be configured to display one or more lists that display records associated to the selected Contact record. 

For performance reasons, the number of records included in the lists may be limited by the System Administrator (configurable option) and only the most recent records will be included in the list.

Some examples of supported lists are listed below:

  • Sales/Support/Service Lists
  • Associated Contacts List
  • Product List
