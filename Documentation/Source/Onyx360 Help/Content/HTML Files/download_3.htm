Download Contacts from Onyx

Contact records in Onyx may be downloaded to the user’s Outlook address book directly from Onyx 360.  All contact records in Onyx that the user is associated with by means of an Internal Contact relationship (type: Outlook Contact Download) will be available to be downloaded locally to the user’s Outlook address book.

Users can view the associated contact records as a list, and select the ones to download from the list.  A preview option is also available so the users can quickly look through the data stored in Onyx before downloading.

Note: Only the contacts that do not already exist in the user’s Outlook address book are displayed in the list and in the preview.
