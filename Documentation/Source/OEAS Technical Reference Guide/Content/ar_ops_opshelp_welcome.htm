Welcome to OPS Administration

OPS Administration is a tool that enables you to manage the OPS service, the fulfillment subsystems, and batch subsystems. You can also view and modify literature order lines and monitor action batches.

For detailed instructions on using this tool, click to access the Help system.

Quick overview

  • Click to refresh the status of the OPS service and subsystems in the navigation area.
  • Click to add a new subsystem or a system document.
  • Click to delete a subsystem or system document. Note that the delete does not occur until you save your changes.
  • Click to start the OPS service or subsystem. You also use this button to continue the service or subsystem after pausing.
  • Click to pause the OPS service or subsystem.
  • Click to stop the OPS service or subsystem.
  • Click to expand the edit area or contract the edit area.
  • Click to set the number of lines returned in the fulfillment and batch queues.
  • Click to save your changes.
