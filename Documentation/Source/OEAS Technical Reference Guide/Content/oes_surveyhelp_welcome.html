Welcome to Survey Administration

The Survey Administration tool enables you to create, modify, activate or deactivate survey types, surveys, survey questions, and possible question responses.

For detailed instructions on using this tool, click to access the Help system.

Quick overview

To view or modify a survey type, survey, question, or response, select it in the navigation area.

  • Click to expand an item within the navigation area.
  • Click to collapse an item within the navigation area.
  • Select an item in the navigation area and click to add a new element that item contains. You can do this in order to add:
  • A new survey to a survey type
  • A new question to a survey or to All Questions
  • A new response to a question or to All Responses
  • Select an item in the navigation area and click to mark it for deletion.
  • In the navigation area, select a question in an expanded survey and click to move it up in the survey’s list of questions.
  • In the navigation area, select a question in an expanded survey and click to move it down in the survey’s list of questions.
  • Click to save your changes.
