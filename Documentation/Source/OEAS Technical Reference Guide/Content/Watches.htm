watches Object

You can mark specific customer incident records to watch. You can subscribe to notifications and receive email alerts when records that you are watching are modified.

Customer and Incident Watch

You can now mark specific customer and incidents records to watch. You can subscribe to notifications and receive email alerts when records that you are watching are modified.

The watches object does not have any properties.
