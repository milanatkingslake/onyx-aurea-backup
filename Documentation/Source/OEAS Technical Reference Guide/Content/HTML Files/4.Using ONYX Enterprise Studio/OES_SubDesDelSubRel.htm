Deleting a subscription relationship

Before deleting a subscription relationship, consider the following:

  • Synchronization continues to support the cascaded subscriptions that occurred prior to the subscription relationship being deleted.
  • Cascaded deletions and unsubscriptions are also no longer supported for the objects of the deleted subscription relationship.

Do not delete a previously active subscription relationship after deployment of OEP-Offline. Once the relationship is deleted, unsubscriptions and deletions are not cascaded to the secondary objects of the relationship, which can result in orphaned records in OEP-Offline. In the case of deletions, the subscription_item_cleanup.sql script removes the orphaned records. In the case of unsubscriptions, you must remove the orphaned records manually.

To delete a subscription relationship:

  1. In the navigation area, locate and select the subscription relationship that you want to delete.
  2. Click to mark the subscription relationship for deletion.

The subscription relationship name appears with a strikethrough font in the navigation area (for example: newSubscriptionRelationship).

  1. Click to save your changes and delete the subscription relationship.

If you have simply marked the subscription relationship for deletion and have not yet saved the changes, you can still “undelete” the subscription relationship by unmarking it for deletion.

Use the following procedure to unmark a subscription relationship marked for deletion.

To unmark a subscription relationship marked for deletion:

  1. In the navigation area, locate and select the subscription relationship that you previously marked for deletion. It is distinguished in the navigation area by the strikethrough font of its name.
  2. Click to unmark the subscription relationship for deletion.

The strikethrough font is removed from the name, and the subscription relationship is unmarked for deletion.

  1. Click to save your changes.
