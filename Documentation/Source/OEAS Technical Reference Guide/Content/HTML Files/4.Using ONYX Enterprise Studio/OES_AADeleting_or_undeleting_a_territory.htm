Deleting and undeleting items

After you mark an item (territory, condition, expression, or assignment set) for deletion and save your changes, you cannot undo the deletion. Additionally, you cannot delete items with associated records. All associations must be deleted before you can delete the item. Use the following procedure to delete or undelete an item in Assignment Administration.

To delete an item (territory, condition, expression, or assignment set):

  1. Select the item you want to delete.
  2. Click to mark the item for deletion.

Items marked for deletion are indicated in the navigation area by names in strike-through font.

  1. Click on the toolbar to save your changes to the OEDB.

To undelete an item marked for deletion:

  1. Select the item that you previously marked for deletion. It is distinguished in the navigation area by the strike-through font of its name.
  2. Click to unmark the item for deletion.
  3. Click to save your changes to the OEDB.
