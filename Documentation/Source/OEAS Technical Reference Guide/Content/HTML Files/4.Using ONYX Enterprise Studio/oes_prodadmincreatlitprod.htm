Creating a literature product

You create literature products using the Literature Information data cell.

Modifying any information on the Literature Information data cell will cause the product to become a literature product. Do not add or modify literature information for the product unless you intend for the product to be a literature product.

To create a literature product:

  1. In the navigation area, expand the product group of the product that you want to make a literature product.
  2. Select the product you want to make a literature product. The attributes for this product appear in the edit area.
  3. Expand the Literature Information data cell by clicking anywhere on the cell header.
  4. In the edit area, define the general attributes for the literature product. See Converting a standard product to a literature product for the list of attributes in the edit area.
  5. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.

Once created, you cannot delete a literature product without deleting the entire product; you can only disable it.
