Modifying a product group

To modify a product group:

  1. In the navigation area, locate and select the product group that you want to modify.

The attributes for this product group display in the edit area.

  1. Apply your modifications to this product group's attributes. See Creating a product group for the list of attributes you can modify in the edit area.
  2. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.
