Moving a product to another product group

There are two ways to move a product to another product group:

  • Using the Parent Group drop-down list in the edit area
  • Using drag-and-drop

To move a product to another product group using the Parent Group drop-down list in the edit area:

  1. In the navigation area, locate and select the product you want to move.
  2. In the edit area, in the Parent Group drop-down list, select the product group to which you want the product moved.
  3. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.

To move a product to another product group using drag-and-drop:

  1. In the navigation area, locate the product you want to move and also the destination product group to which you want to move the product.
  2. Click the product you want to move, drag it to the destination product group to which you want to move it, and drop it within that group.

There are two options for where you can drop a product when you are moving it from one product group to another

  • If the destination product group node is contracted, you must drop the group you are moving directly onto the destination product group node within the navigation tree.
  • If the destination product group node is expanded (displaying child groups and/or products), you can drop the product you are moving on any of the following:
  • The destination production group node within the navigation tree.
  • The static Groups node directly under the destination product group.
  • The Products node under the group, or, any product within the Products node.
  1. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.

When moving a product using the Parent Group drop-down list, the new location of the product is not reflected within the navigation area until you have saved your changes.
