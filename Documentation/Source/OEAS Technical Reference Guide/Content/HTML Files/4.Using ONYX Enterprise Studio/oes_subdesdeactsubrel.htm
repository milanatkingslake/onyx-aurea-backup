Activating or deactivating a subscription relationship

Before deactivating a subscription relationship, consider the following:

  • Synchronization continues to support the cascaded subscriptions that occurred prior to the subscription relationship being deactivated.
  • Cascaded deletions and unsubscriptions are also no longer supported for the objects of the deactivated subscription relationship.

To activate or deactivate a subscription relationship:

  1. In the navigation area, locate and select the subscription relationship you want to modify.

The attributes of this subscription relationship appear in the edit area.

  1. Select or clear the Active check box to activate or deactivate the subscription relationship.
  2. Click to save your changes.
