Copying an incident product

To copy an incident product:

  1. In the navigation area, locate the incident product you want to copy and also the destination incident category to which you want to copy the incident product.
  2. Click the incident product you want to copy, drag it to the destination incident category to which you want to copy it, and drop it within that category.
  3. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.

This process copies the incident product to another incident category. It does not move the original incident product from its incident category. To remove an incident product from an incident category, delete the incident product.
