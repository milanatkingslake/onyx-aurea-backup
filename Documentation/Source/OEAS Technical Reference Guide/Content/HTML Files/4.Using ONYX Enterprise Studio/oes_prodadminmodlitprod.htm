Working with literature products

Literature products are special types of standard products that have functional attributes in addition to those of a product, such as the ability to be sent directly to a printer. This additional information is created in the Literature Information data cell of a product, and the literature product is created the moment you modify the attributes of this data cell. Literature products are used to complete Literature Order records.
