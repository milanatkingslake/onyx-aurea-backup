Modifying a standard product

To modify a standard product:

  1. In the navigation area, navigate to and expand the product group of the product you want to modify.
  2. Select the product you want to modify.
  3. In the edit area, apply your modifications to the general attributes for this product. See Creating a standard product for the list of attributes you can modify.
  4. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.
