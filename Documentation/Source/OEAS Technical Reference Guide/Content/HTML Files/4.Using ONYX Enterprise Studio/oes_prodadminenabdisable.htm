Activating or deactivating a literature product

Modifying any information on the Literature Information data cell will cause the product to become a literature product. Do not add or modify literature information for the product unless you intend for the product to be a literature product.

To activate or deactivate a literature product:

  1. In the navigation area, navigate to and expand the product group of the literature product you want enable or disable.
  2. Select the literature product you want to modify. The attributes for this literature product appear in the edit area.
  3. Expand the Literature Information data cell by clicking anywhere on the cell header. This literature product's literature information appears in the edit area.
  4. Check or uncheck the Active Literature box to enable or disable this literature product. A checked box implies an active literature product that will be returned as an orderable item for Literature Order records.
  5. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.
