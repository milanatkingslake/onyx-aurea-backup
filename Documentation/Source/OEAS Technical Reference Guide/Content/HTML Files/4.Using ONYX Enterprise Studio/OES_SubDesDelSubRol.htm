Deleting a subscription role

Before deleting a subscription role, consider that synchronization continues to support the cascaded subscriptions that occurred prior to the subscription role being deleted.

To delete a subscription role:

  1. In the navigation area, locate and select the subscription role that you want to delete.
  2. Click to mark the subscription role for deletion.

The subscription role name appears with a strikethrough font in the navigation area (for example: newSubscriptionRole).

  1. Click to save your changes and delete the subscription role.

If you have simply marked the subscription role for deletion and have not yet saved the changes, you can still “undelete” the subscription role by unmarking it for deletion.

To unmark a subscription role marked for deletion:

  1. In the navigation area, locate and select the subscription role that you previously marked for deletion. It is distinguished in the navigation area by the strikethrough font of its name.
  2. Click to unmark the subscription role for deletion.

The strikethrough font is removed from the name, and the subscription role is unmarked for deletion.

  1. Click to save your changes.
