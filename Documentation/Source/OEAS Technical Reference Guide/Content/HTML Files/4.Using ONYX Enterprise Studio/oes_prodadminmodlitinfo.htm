Modifying literature product information

Modifying any information on the Literature Information data cell will cause the product to become a literature product. Do not add or modify literature information for the product unless you intend for the product to be a literature product.

To modify literature product information:

  1. In the navigation area, navigate to and expand the product group of the product you want to modify.
  2. Select the product you want to modify.
  3. Expand the Literature Information data cell by clicking anywhere on the cell header.
  4. In the edit area, apply your modifications to the literature attributes for this product. See Creating a literature product for the list of attributes you can modify in the edit area.
  5. Click on the toolbar to save your changes.
