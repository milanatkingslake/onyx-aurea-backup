Products

Within Onyx Enterprise Application Server (OEAS), products is a general term that encompasses three different kinds of products:

  • Standard products
  • Literature products
  • Incident products

These three types of products are domain data for three separate features of OEAS. The following descriptions of these products are based on the default implementation of OEAS.

Standard Products - Standard products, are used to complete customer products, forecasts, and quotes.

Literature products - Literature products are used to create literature order objects. Literature products are special types of standard products that have additional functional attributes such as the ability for the product to be sent directly to a printer. You add these additional attributes to a standard product in Product Administration, in the Literature Information data cell for the product.

Literature products are also used to create customer product, and forecast and quote records. However, in the context of the Customer Products feature, the additional functional attributes of the literature product are ignored.

Incident products - Incident products are used to facilitate tracking and categorization of incidents and work tickets. You create incident products separately from standard products and literature products, and you can use them independent of the other products. Optionally, you can relate one or more standard products to an incident product using Product Administration. Doing so facilitates tracking the incidents as they relate to actual products.

OEAS enables you to organize your standard products and literature products by product families or groups, which can benefit companies with large product offerings. Incident products are organized by incident category types (sales, service, support, and work ticket).

  • Standard products, including those with literature attributes, are included in customer product records.
  • Customer products can have any number of internal or external contacts.
  • Customer products can have any number of file attachments.
  • A standard product can be associated with any number of forecast or quote line items.
  • Standard products, including those with literature attributes, can be associated with any number of customer product, and forecast and quote records.
  • A literature product can be associated with any number of literature order line items.
  • An incident product can be related to any number of incidents that are of the same incident category as the incident product.
  • A work ticket (category) incident product can be related to any number of work tickets.
  • A literature product can be associated with any number of other literature products as a “label product” when its “is label” flag is set to true.
  • A standard product can be associated with any number of tags.
