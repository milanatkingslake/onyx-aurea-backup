Relationships

This book contains the object and table relationships for customers.

When reviewing the object and table relationships for customers, note that:

  • A company can be related to many individuals, but an individual can be related to only one company (excluding an individualâ€™s relationship to a company as an external contact).
  • A company can have only one primary contact.
  • Customers, both companies and individuals, can have any number of customer product records, literature orders, incidents, surveys, addresses, phones, and attachments.
  • Customers, both companies and individuals, can be related to a work ticket.
  • A customer can have only one alert.
  • A company can belong to only one company family.
  • A company or an individual can have any number of tags.
