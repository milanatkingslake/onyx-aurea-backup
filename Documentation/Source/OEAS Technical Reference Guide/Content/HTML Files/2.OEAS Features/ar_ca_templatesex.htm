Example use of the Templates feature

The following example demonstrates how you might use the Templates feature to support your company’s customer relationships by enabling timely customer service responses.

Example: Customer support

Suppose there is a company, Global TelCom, that develops, manages, and operates broadband cable networks and provides programming content.

Global TelCom’s support representatives:

  • Receive phone calls from existing customers who are experiencing service problems.
  • Create support incidents based on the customer’s description of the problem and assign a support technician to the incident to research and resolve the problem.

Global TelCom’s support technicians:

  • Follow up with the customer to further identify the problem.
  • Work with the customer to resolve the problem.
  • Close the support incident once the problem has been resolved.
