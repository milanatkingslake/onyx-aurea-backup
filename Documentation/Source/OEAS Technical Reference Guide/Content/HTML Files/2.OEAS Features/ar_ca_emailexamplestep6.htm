Step 6: Sales representative closes the deal

The sales representative performs the following tasks:

  • Discusses the purchase with the individual over the phone. The discussion is notated on the sales incident.
  • Completes the purchase and closes the sales incident.
  • Responds to the individual’s email by writing a “thank you” reply message from within OEP.
  • Before sending the reply email, adds several literature item attachments requested by the individual.
  • Sends the “thank you” email to the individual.

In OEAS:

  • Because the “thank you” email is a reply to the previous email sent by the individual, all non-contact associations to the individual, company, and incident records that were included in the previous email are also created for the “thank you” email.

In the end-user interface:

  • The email appears in the individual, company, and sales incident history pages and in the email thread view.
