Step 5: Customer replies to the sales representative

The individual customer sends a reply to the sales representative’s personal email account, requesting a phone call to discuss the purchase. The sales representative uses one of the menu options on the Save in Onyx toolbar to record and track the email in the database.

In OEAS:

  • The email is forwarded to the monitored mailbox created for Onyx Email for Outlook.
  • EMS finds the email message and assimilates it into OEAS.
  • EMS uses the tracking information in the message to create a link to the email thread and to create the non-contact associations to the customer and incident records.
  • EMS uses the sender and recipient names included with the email to create contact associations for the individual and the sales representative (internal user).

In the end-user interface:

  • The individual’s reply email message appears on the individual, company, and sales incident history pages and in the email thread view.
