Campaigns

The Campaign feature is the OEAS functionality that enables you to gather information about your customers and how they interact with your business.

For example, you can use campaigns to:

  • Determine which of your advertising efforts attracts the most business.
  • Discover and track which customers attended your last conference.
  • Discover and track which customers are purchasing a certain kind of product.
  • Define milestones to manage the specific actions required to meet the goals of a campaign.
