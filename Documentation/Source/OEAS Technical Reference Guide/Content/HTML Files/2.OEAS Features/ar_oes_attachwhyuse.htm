Why use attachments?

The ability to attach files of all types is critical to workflow for most businesses. For example, users in technical support need to attach files, code samples, and knowledge-base articles to their support incidents, while sales users need to attach copies of contracts, responses, and competitive information to customer records.

Following are typical examples:

Example: Sales opportunity

A customer purchases stock through a broker. On confirmation of the purchase, the broker sends the trade confirmation document out to the customer, and attaches the document to the Sales Opportunity for later reference.

Example: Support incident

A customer calls to report an error in a software product he or she purchased. The support technician creates a support incident for the customer, attaches the software patch to the incident form, and sends the form to the customer. The support incident is closed.
