Example use of the Email feature

The following example demonstrates how you might use the Email feature to support your company’s customer relationships.

Example: Sales group at Chocolate Inc.

Suppose a sales group for Chocolate Inc., an online chocolate retailer, uses the Email feature to handle their customer correspondence.

Chocolate Inc. sales representatives’ tasks include taking phone calls from customers and promoting products on the Chocolate Inc. Web site using a corporate alias. The sales representatives’ uses of OEP and Onyx Email for Outlook could include the following:

  • Using Task Manager to search for emails marked for follow-up and their assigned sales incidents.
  • Using Onyx Email for Outlook to record and track emails in OEAS.

Using the Email feature, Chocolate Inc. can quickly respond to customer requests, track email threads, and close deals.

Following are the steps involved:

  • Step 1: Interested customer sends an email to the corporate alias
  • Step 2: Task Manager searches for inbound flagged emails
  • Step 3: Task Manager searches for sales incidents
  • Step 4: Sales manager sends a reply to the sales representative
  • Step 5: Customer replies to the sales representative
  • Step 6: Sales representative closes the deal
