Email address book item entries (queues)

Email address book item entries, referred to as queues in OEP, are used by the Email feature to populate the From box of an email message. Queues make it easy for inbound reply messages from companies or individuals to be sent directly to a monitored corporate mailbox, such as a sales or support mailbox, for processing by an email monitor subsystem.

You use Table Administration to create queues for your company. Use OPS Administration to configure corporate mailboxes to receive inbound mail sent to the queues.
