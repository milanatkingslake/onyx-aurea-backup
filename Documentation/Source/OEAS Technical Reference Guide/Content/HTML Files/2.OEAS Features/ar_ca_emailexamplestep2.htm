Step 2: Task Manager searches for inbound flagged emails

The sales manager uses Task Manager to search for all inbound emails flagged for follow-up, and performs the following tasks:

  • Creates a sales incident for the email from the individual customer and links the email to the sales incident.
  • Creates a non-contact association (link) to the individual and his or her company. The link enables all emails in the thread to appear on the individual record and company record (even if the individual or company is not a sender or recipient of a particular email in the thread).
  • The manager assigns the sales incident to a sales representative for follow-up with the individual.

In the end-user interface:

  • The email message appears on the individual, company, and sales incident history pages.
