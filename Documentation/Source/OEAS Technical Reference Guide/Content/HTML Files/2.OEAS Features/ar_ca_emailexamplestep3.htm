Step 3: Task Manager searches for sales incidents

A sales representative uses Tasks Manager to search for assigned sales incidents. He or she sees this sales incident related to the individual customer and does the following:

  • Uses the Email tab to open the email.
  • Sends a reply to the individual from the sales representativeâ€™s personal email account (not the corporate mailbox), including the manager of the sales group as a bcc recipient.

In the end-user interface, the email:

  • Contains all non-contact links related to the previous email.
  • Appears on the individual, company, and sales incident history lists.
  • Includes both emails in its email thread view.
