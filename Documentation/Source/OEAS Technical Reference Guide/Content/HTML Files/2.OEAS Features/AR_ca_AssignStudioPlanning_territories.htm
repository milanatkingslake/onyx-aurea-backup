Planning territories

A territory assignment structure typically consists of one or more territories, conditions, assignment sets, and users. This data is flexible and can be organized to support various business models. Your structure should match your organization's business model (rules) for assigning leads to customers and incidents.

When you plan your territory structure, consider the following:

  • Internal contacts for customers and incidents.
  • Incident territories for the assignment of incidents.
  • Nested territories and prioritized processing for organizational flexibility.
  • Territories and assignment sets based on a business model developed from your business rules. For more information on these concepts, see the sample business model.
  • Assignment Studio process that fulfill the needs of your business model.
