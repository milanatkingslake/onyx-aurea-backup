Common terms

The following is a list of terms that are related to the Multiple Currency feature.

Base Currency - The currency you assign to a product when you add the product to the Onyx Enterprise Database (OEDB) using Product Administration.

Euro - European Monetary Unit (EMU). The currency created by the European Commission.

National Currency - The local currency of a country that is a member of the EMU.

Primary Currency - The first currency of a forecast or quote. This is the currency on which conversions are calculated for the secondary currency of the forecast or quote.

Rounding - Related to Euro conversions, rounding regulations pertain to the limitations on the precision of Euro conversion calculations.

Secondary Currency - The second currency an end user selects for a forecast or quote so that they can save a forecast or quote in two distinct currencies.
