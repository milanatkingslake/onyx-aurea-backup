What is Assignment Studio?

The Assignment Studio is a system for designing and implementing territory and lead assignments for OEP. The Assignment Administration tool within Assignment Studio enables you to define and implement simple conditions (rules) that are used to do the following:

  • Assign companies and individuals to territories

You can create conditions to assign a company or individual to a particular territory based on properties of the company or individual object and their associated primary phone and address.

For example, a regional account sales representative could be assigned to certain customers who live in different geographic regions within the United States. In this case, the customer is associated with a particular territory based on the regionCode property.

  • Assign incidents to users

You can create conditions to assign an incident to a particular person. Incident assignment is based on the properties of the incident object, and optionally, the properties of the incident's owning customer record.

For example, a support representative could be assigned responsibility for incoming support incidents, based on the incident product purchased by a customer. When a new support incident is created, the product property of the incident is examined, and the incident is assigned to the support representative based on the incident assignment conditions.

  • Create contacts to associate with company or individual conditions

You can create contacts to associate with company or individual conditions based on the company or individual's territory assignment.

For example, a sales representative is assigned responsibility for all companies within a territory. When a new company is saved into the Onyx database, the appropriate sales representative's internal contact type is created based on the company assignment conditions.

  • Create contacts to associate with incident conditions

You can create contacts to associate with incident conditions based on properties of the incident object, and optionally, the owning customer's territory assignment.

For example, a group of service representatives could be assigned responsibility for customers within a territory based on the incident product. When a new service incident is created in the Onyx database, the product property of the incident is examined, and the appropriate service representative is created as an internal contact type for the incident. Selection of internal contacts is based on the incident assignment conditions for the territory.
