Step 1: Interested customer sends an email to the corporate alias

Using their native email client such as Eudora, an individual expresses interest in a new product in an email that he or she sends to the corporate alias posted on the company Web site.

When the individualâ€™s email arrives, the following happens in OEAS and OPS:

  • The email monitor subsystem (EMS) finds the email in the corporate mailbox and imports it into OEAS.
  • The EMS creates a contact association to the individual based on the email address from which the email was sent.
  • The email is flagged for follow-up as specified through the EMS settings in OPS Administration.
