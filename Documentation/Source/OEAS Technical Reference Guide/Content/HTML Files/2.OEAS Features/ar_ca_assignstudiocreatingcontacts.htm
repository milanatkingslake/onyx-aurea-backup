Creating contacts for customers and incidents

When creating contacts for customers and incidents, consider the following:

  • Internal contacts for customers and incidents are automatically created on the insert of customer and incident records in the database.
  • During the update of customer records, only the association between a company or individual and the associated territory is updated.
  • During the update of incident records, the association between the incident and the associated territory is updated and the incident is optionally re-assigned. For more information, see Assigning incidents.
  • Unlike the company and individual update process, the incident update process is not configured to run on every incident update. By default, it only runs when an OEP user manually assigns an incident to the Auto Assign user. For more information, see the Manual assignment of incidents.
