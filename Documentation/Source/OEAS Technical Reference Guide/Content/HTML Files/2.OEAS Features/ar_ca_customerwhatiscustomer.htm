Customers

The Onyx Enterprise Application Server (OEAS) is comprised of numerous CRM features and, because OEAS is designed primarily to help you manage, maintain, and support your customer relationship management (CRM) activities, nearly all of these features are either directly or indirectly related to the Customer feature.

There are two types of customers:

  • Companies
  • Individuals

In general, multiple individuals are logically related to one company since there are many individuals working for one company. With OEAS, you have the flexibility to enter separate data for both companies and individuals, as well as to establish relationships between them.

In the default OEAS implementation, customers can own customer product, incident, and literature order records, and you can relate customers to each other in two ways: you can relate a single individual to a company as that company™s primary contact, and you can relate a company to any number of individuals. By establishing relationships between companies and individuals, you can quickly access information about a company, its employees and their job titles.

For more information regarding internal and external contacts, see Contacts .
