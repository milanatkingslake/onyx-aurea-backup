Surveys

Surveys are information-gathering tools that can help you collect targeted information about your customers and therefore more effectively serve them. Among the numerous benefits of well-administered surveys are more accurately targeted campaigns and increased customer input on future product development.

For example, with surveys, customers can provide information about themselves by answering questions about:

  • Their level of expertise with your company’s products
  • The products they use, and how they use them
  • Which markets they're targeting
  • Their future interests in products
  • Personal information
  • Company size

A survey that you create with the Survey feature is a set of questions that might have a hierarchical structure, and the responses associated with those questions. When respondents take the survey, their answers are associated with both the questions and the respondents.

  • You create surveys and their questions with the Survey Administration tool in Onyx Enterprise Studio (OES).
  • Surveys and their data, questions, and possible responses reside in the Onyx Enterprise Database (OEDB).
  • Your employees or customers can enter answers with Onyx Employee Portal (OEP) or Onyx Customer Portal (OCP).

You can also create a custom application for entering respondents’ answers to a survey.
