Email Literature Products

Email literature products are literature products configured with one or more of the following email fulfillment types:

  • Send as Email Body (supports merging)
  • Send as Email Attachment (supports merging)
  • Send as Email Attachment (no merging)

Using the Literature feature, users can order email literature products which are fulfilled via email. Email literature products are created from XSL templates, and support data merging. Sample templates are installed during the OPS installation. You can customize them to create personalized email messages.
