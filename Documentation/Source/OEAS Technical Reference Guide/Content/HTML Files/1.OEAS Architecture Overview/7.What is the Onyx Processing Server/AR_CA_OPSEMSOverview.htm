Email Monitor Subsystem

The email monitor subsystem (EMS) monitors Microsoft Exchange mailboxes for inbound email that is related to an Onyx record (the inbound email is a reply to email sent from OEAS), and for saving the email to OEDB. Using certain information in the email header, such as the sender and recipient email addresses and an internal thread identifier, the EMS subsystem associates inbound mail with companies, individuals, and Onyx users. Using these associations, inbound emails are made available on customer and incident history lists.

EMS continuously monitors mailboxes on designated mail servers for inbound emails. For email addresses that are publicized via Websites or print media, for instance, you can configure EMS to examine mailboxes for inbound emails and to insert the emails (along with their attachments) into the OEDB.
