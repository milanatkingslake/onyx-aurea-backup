Configuring currency codes

You use currency codes when you create or modify products in Product Administration. The currency types (codes) are displayed in a drop-down list within the Inventory Information edit area when you have selected a product in the navigation area. You can associate a currency with a product, and when that product is selected for a forecast or quote record, the currency you associated with that product controls the international display of currency and the currency conversion required for the forecast or quote record.

For complete details regarding the currency domain data, see Forecasts and Quotes.
