Step 2: Determine your literature products and subsystems

There are three types of literature products:

General literature products — Users can order these literature products by using the Literature feature. Examples include a customer thank-you letter or email, a company brochure, or a software program.

Mailing labels — These literature products are configured as mailing labels and associated with other general literature products. When the general literature product is ordered, OPS automatically generates the associated mailing label.

System documents — These literature products are configured to be generated when batches of order lines are processed. Examples include order summaries or packing slips.

Your literature product's fulfillment type determines the subsystems that will be needed to fulfill the orders.
