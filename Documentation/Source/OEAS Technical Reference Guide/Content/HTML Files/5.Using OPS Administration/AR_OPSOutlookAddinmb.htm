Adding and configuring a mailbox for Onyx Email for Outlook

The Onyx Email for Outlook is a plug-in for Microsoft Outlook with which end-users can save emails into the database from their personal Outlook inboxes and track new emails sent from their personal account in OEP. When an email arrives, the Email for Outlook does the following:

  • Creates a new email and then attaches the original email to the new email.
  • Forwards the email to a mailbox that is monitored by an EMS.
  • The EMS processes and saves the email into the database.

A monitored mailbox that receives email from the Email for Outlook requires a special setting in the Edit Mailbox area: the Message Type field must be set to Save in Onyx Format. A monitored mailbox must receive only one type of email: either standard email messages or Email for Outlook messages.

Use the procedure Adding and configuring monitored mailboxes to configure a mailbox to receive Email for Outlook email messages.
