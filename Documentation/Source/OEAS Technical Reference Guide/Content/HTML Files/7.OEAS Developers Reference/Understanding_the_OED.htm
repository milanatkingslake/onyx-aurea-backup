Understanding the OED

Understanding the Onyx Enterprise Dictionary (OED) is the most important step toward developing customizations for the OEAS. The topics in this section provide the necessary information to understand the role of the OED in the OEAS architecture and to develop custom solutions on this platform. This section includes the following topic categories.

  • OED concepts: Conceptual overview of the metadata in the OED
  • Object creation guidelines: Conceptual discussion of customizing the OED
  • Object creation scenarios: Step by step explanations of creating customizations for three different object categories
  • Managing the OED: Conceptual topics on going live with your customizations
