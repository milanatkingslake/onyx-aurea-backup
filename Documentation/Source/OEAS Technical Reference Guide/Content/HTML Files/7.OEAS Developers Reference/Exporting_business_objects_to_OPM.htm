Exporting business objects into OPM

The Export to OPM feature enables Onyx Process Manager (OPM) administrators or developers, who are responsible for configuring and maintaining OPM, to export OEAS business objects into OPM. The export functionality translates objects from the working copy of the OED into packages in the OPM library. Once these are available within the OPM Process Designer tool, the work related to process building can begin.

  • Before you use this feature, OPM must be installed and configured. See the OPM System Administrator's Guide for instructions.
  • Before you re-export objects into the OPM package library, there are some considerations to be aware of. For more information, see Re-exporting OEAS business objects into OPM.

To export OEAS business objects into the Onyx Process Manager:

  1. From the main toolbar, click . The Export to OPM dialog box appears.
  2. In the OPM Target Application Name field, enter the OPM application name that you want the objects to be associated with.
  1. In the Business Objects to Export list box, select one or more objects to export into OPM.
  2. Click the Export button. You can cancel the export process at any point by closing the window or clicking the Close button.

If the export process succeeds without errors, a success note appears in the status field. However, if it finds any problems during the integrity check, explanations of those problems display in a summary window.

Warning: In the Business Objects to Export list, you can multi-select objects as follows: To select adjacent objects, click the first object, and then hold down SHIFT and click the last object. To select nonadjacent objects, click the first object, and then hold down CTRL and click an additional object.
