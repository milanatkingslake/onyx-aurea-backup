Understanding validation

The Object Designer performs two types of validation on metadata: partial validation and extensive validation. The validation process checks your modifications with the Onyx XML schema (OnyxEnterpriseDictionarySchema.xml) and ensures the following:

  • References, directly or indirectly, are not made to non-existent, invalid, or disabled items.
  • There are no circular references.
  • Cross-dependency issues do not exist

Important: The validation process does not check whether business objects and step components require the existence of another item. Before you publish your modifications, carefully test them in an OEAS test environment before implementing them in your production environment.
