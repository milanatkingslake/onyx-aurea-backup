About Logical Business Objects

Logical Business Objects

The logical business objects collection is the parent collection that holds all metadata describing the logical structures on the business tier, as well as the logical-physical information that associate entities in the business tier with entities in the data tier.

Each business object is an entity that can (1) hold data and/or (2) perform work. OEAS business objects are objects in the true OOP sense. They encapsulate data in properties and expose methods to perform work related to the data they hold and the relationships they have with other objects. Individual attributes of an object are a unique name, a description, and some other information mostly related to validity and version control. In this release of the OED, object inheritance is not supported.
