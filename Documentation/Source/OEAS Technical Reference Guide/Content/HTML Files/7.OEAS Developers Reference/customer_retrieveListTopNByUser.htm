retrieveListTopNByUser (customer)

retrieveListTopNByUser method

The retrieveListTopNByUser method of the customer object returns a list of the top "N" customers most recently updated by a specified user. The number of customers, "N", is specified by the rowsPerType parameter. The data is returned as a rowSet object in the XML.

Remarks

Customers that are either marked as private or as deleted are not returned.

The returned list of the top "N" most recently updated customers meets the following criteria:

  • The userId is an internal contact, of any type, on the individual or company
  • The individual or company is one of the "N" most recently updated. "N" is specified by the rowsPerType parameter. (For example, if N=10, 10 companies and 10 individuals are returned)
  • Update date on the customer is defined as the greater of the update date on any incident or task record or the associated work notes for which the customer is the owner. (Customers without incidents and tasks are not returned)
