Deleting an email monitor subsystem

When you delete a subsystem, all mailboxes that belong to the subsystem are also deleted.

To delete a subsystem:

  1. In the navigation area, select the subsystem that you want to delete.
  2. Click to stop the subsystem.
  3. Click .

    4. In the navigation area, the name of the subsystem is struck through until you save your changes.

  4. Click to save your changes.
