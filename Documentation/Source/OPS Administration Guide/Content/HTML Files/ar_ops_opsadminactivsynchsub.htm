Activating or deactivating a synchronization subsystem

Activating and deactivating a sync subsystem enables and disables synchronization for all the OEP-Offline users on that subsystem.

To activate or deactivate a sync subsystem:

  1. In the navigation area of OPS Administration, select the sync subsystem that you want to activate or deactivate.
  2. Select or remove the selection of the Active attribute, according to whether you want to make the subsystem active or inactive.
