Deleting a subsystem

You cannot delete a subsystem that has already processed order lines.

To delete a subsystem:

  1. In the navigation area, select the subsystem that you want to delete.
  2. Click to stop the subsystem.
  3. Click on the toolbar.

    4. The subsystem appears crossed out in the navigation area until you save your changes.

  4. Click to save your changes.
